The game Togyz Kumalak uses a special board
with two rows of small holes (nine in each row)
and two large holes, which are called "Kazan".

At the beginning of the game in each small hole is
nine stones each.

The player wins the game if he collects in his cauldron.
More stones than the opponent collects in his own.

If at the end of the game, each of the players has
the same number of stones, then the game ends in a draw.
